# brainstorming

## Procedure

1. Restate the feature objective and constraints.
2. Propose 2-4 viable implementation options.
3. Evaluate each option on complexity, risk, performance, and delivery speed.
4. Choose one option with explicit rationale.
5. Record rejected options and why they were rejected.
